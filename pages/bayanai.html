BayanAI

Royal Society of Art x Google Deep Mind Design Award

May 2024

Overview

BayanAI is a climate adaptation solution that leverages artificial intelligence to help communities in the Philippines better prepare for and respond to climate-related disasters.

Challenge

The Philippines faces increasing climate-related disasters, with limited resources for effective disaster preparedness and response.

Solution

BayanAI combines local knowledge with AI technology to create a more resilient disaster response system.

Impact

BayanAI aims to improve disaster preparedness and response times while empowering local communities.
