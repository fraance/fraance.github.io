1. e-ROUTES App for EV Cars co-pilote

To improve the e-Routes app, I conducted four comprehensive benchmarks, focusing on industry best practices for map-based visualizations. My research identified critical use case patterns, especially relevant for visualizing charging station locations for electric vehicles.

Through my findings, I presented actionable insights and original design proposals that contributed to refining the UI and UX of the e-Routes app.

e-ROUTES available on Playstore and Apple Store

2. Space App - A Unified Platform

The Space App project aimed to unify the Stellantis portfolio by consolidating 14 individual brand-specific apps into a single platform, providing a streamlined user experience.

As part of the UX team, I managed and conducted over 40 usability tests. My focus was on enhancing three core features:

  • Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS)
  • EV Scheduling
  • Remote Control Functionality and Geo-Fencing

The comprehensive feedback reports I provided led to significant improvements in the app's usability and user experience across multiple functionalities.

3. UX and UI Design for Stellantis Multibrand Design System

As Stellantis adopted a unified multibrand design system, I contributed to streamlining the design process, which simplified the challenges associated with creating cohesive experiences across a broad, diverse vehicle portfolio.

Key Responsibilities: Developed wireframes and mockups for mobile interfaces supporting core functionalities, such as calling, favorite settings, and radio control.

Collaboration: Worked closely with senior designers and engineering managers to ensure seamless integration with vehicle systems.

I also took on the role of "brand skinner," where I adapted the design system for Opel, customizing aspects like visual style, typefaces, and motion design while maintaining consistency with the established component architecture.

4. UX Design for Citroën Ami Mobile App

One of my unique challenges involved integrating radio functionality into Citroën Ami's mobile interface, ensuring compatibility with both DAB and FM technologies. Through user-centered design, I developed an intuitive and engaging user interface, enhancing My Ami Play, a feature that supports on-board connectivity and infotainment.

Impact: The user-friendly interface I designed is now deployed in the Citroën Ami, enhancing the overall user experience for drivers.

Each project has broadened my skills in UX design, research, and collaborative problem-solving across a variety of digital and automotive interfaces. I'm proud to have contributed to creating valuable, user-centered solutions that enhance Stellantis's digital ecosystem.
