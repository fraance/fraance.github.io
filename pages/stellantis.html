What I have learned

As an apprentice at Stellantis, I gained a wealth of knowledge and experience in UX and UI design. Some of the key things I learned during my apprenticeship include:

  • I improved my understanding of the UX design process, including the importance of exploring, prototyping, testing, iterating, and delivering high-quality designs.
  • I developed my skills in working with complex design systems, including proficiency in typography, desktop/mobile UI, color, layout, iconography, and aesthetic sense. I also learned how to create responsive designs that work well across different languages and devices.
  • I learned the value of sharing my work early and often with a wide range of people, including colleagues, stakeholders, and users. This helped me to get feedback and refine my designs in the right direction.
  • I learned how to effectively communicate with cross-functional teams and stakeholders, and the importance of keeping them engaged throughout the design process. This helped me to create designs that were coherent, credible, and useful to all parties involved.
  • I had the opportunity to work in a complex, cross-disciplinary team, where I learned how to collaborate effectively with people from different backgrounds and specialties. This helped me to develop my skills in communication, problem-solving, and team-building.

Overall, my apprenticeship at Stellantis was a valuable and enriching experience that helped me grow as a designer and a professional. I am grateful for the opportunity to have learned from experienced colleagues and to have contributed to the success of the company.
