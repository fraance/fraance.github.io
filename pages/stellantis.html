My Ami Play

May 2023 - July 2023

Role

Citroën Ami Mobile App - Responsible for the design and interface integration of My Ami Play.

Process

  • Analyzed user requirements specifically for driving mode functionality.
  • Designed user flows and interactive prototypes to streamline app usage.
